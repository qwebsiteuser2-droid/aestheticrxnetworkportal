## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 What is BioAestheticAx Network?

BioAestheticAx Network is a B2B platform designed for medical clinics and doctors. It allows you to:
- Order medical products
- Share and view research papers
- Track your performance and achievements
- Manage your clinic profile
- Connect with other medical professionals
- **Book appointments with doctors** (for regular users)
- **Receive appointment requests** (for doctors)
- **See doctor online status** in real-time

### 1.2 Accessing the Website

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the website URL (provided by your administrator)
3. You'll see the homepage with information about the platform

## 2. User Types & Access

Our platform supports four types of users, each with different features and access levels.

### 2.1 Doctor 👨‍⚕️
**Who it's for:** Medical professionals and clinic owners

**Features:**
- ✅ Order medical products
- ✅ Access to leaderboard and rankings
- ✅ Earn certificates and achievements
- ✅ Upload and view research papers
- ✅ Track sales and tier progress
- ✅ Manage clinic profile
- ✅ View Hall of Pride

- ✅ **Bell notifications** for appointment updates

**Requirements:**
- No Signup ID needed
- Auto-approved upon registration

### 2.3 Employee 👷

**Features:**
- ✅ View assigned orders

- ✅ Track delivery progress
- ✅ Employee dashboard

**Requirements:**
- No Signup ID needed

### 2.4 Admin 👑

## 3. Account Management

### 3.1 Sign Up Process

#### 3.1.1 For Doctors

1. **Click "Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. **Select "Doctor"** as your user type
3. **Fill in the registration form:**
   - Email address
   - Password (strong password required)
   - Doctor/Clinic name
   - **Signup ID** (provided by admin, e.g., 42001-42030)
   - WhatsApp number (optional)
   - Clinic location (using Google Maps)
   - Consent to terms

4. **Submit the form**
5. **Wait for admin approval** (you'll receive an email notification)
6. **Once approved**, you can log in and start using the platform

#### 3.1.2 For Regular Users

1. **Click "Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. **Select "Regular User"** as your user type
3. **Fill in the registration form:**
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Name
   - Consent to terms

4. **Submit the form**
5. **You're immediately approved** and can log in right away!

#### 3.1.3 For Employees

1. **Click "Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. **Select "Employee"** as your user type
3. **Fill in the registration form:**
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Name
   - Contact information

4. **Submit the form**
5. **You're immediately approved** and can log in
6. **Admin will assign you** to delivery tasks

### 3.2 Login Process

#### 3.2.1 How to Log In

**Option 1: Email and Password**
1. **Click "Login"** on the homepage
2. **Enter your credentials:**
   - Email address (the one you used to sign up)
   - Password
3. **Click "Sign In"**
4. **You'll be redirected** to your dashboard

**Option 2: Sign In with Google** 🆕
1. **Click "Login"** on the homepage
2. **Click "Sign in with Google"** button
3. **Select your Google account** from the popup
4. **Grant permission** to access basic profile info
5. **You'll be automatically logged in** or registered if new user

**Note:** If you don't have an account yet or enter incorrect credentials, you will be automatically redirected to the signup page to create a new account. This helps streamline the registration process for new users.

#### 3.2.2 Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for password reset instructions
4. Follow the link to reset your password

#### 3.2.3 First Time Login

- If you're a **Doctor** and haven't been approved yet, you'll see a "Pending Approval" message
- Check your email for approval notifications
- Once approved, you can access all features

### 3.3 Signup ID System

**What is a Signup ID?**
- A unique code required for Doctor registration
- Provided by administrators
- Each ID can only be used once
- Range: 42001-42030 (example range)

**How to get a Signup ID:**
- Contact your platform administrator
- Request a Signup ID for your clinic
- Admin will provide you with an available ID

**Doctor ID Assignment:**
- After registration, you'll receive a unique **Doctor ID**
- This ID starts from 42001 and increments automatically
- Used for tracking orders, sales, and leaderboard rankings

## 4. User Features

### 4.1 Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Profile Overview**: Your basic information
- **Recent Orders**: Latest order history
- **Tier Status**: Current tier and progress
- **Quick Stats**: Sales, orders, achievements
- **Notifications**: Important updates

### 4.2 Profile Management

#### 4.2.1 Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your name/profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown
3. View your profile information

#### 4.2.2 Profile Sections

Your profile page includes the following sections:

- **📝 Bio**: Personal description and information
- **🏷️ Tags**: Custom tags for categorization
- **📞 Contact**: WhatsApp number and clinic information
- **🏅 Certificates**: All tier achievement certificates (auto-generated)
- **🔬 Research Papers**: Your published research papers
- **💎 Badges**: Achievement badges assigned by admins
- **📈 Rank Progress**: Live tier progress data with points to next rank

#### 4.2.3 Editing Your Profile

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click "Edit Profile" (if you're viewing your own profile)
3. Update information:
   - **Profile photo**: Click to upload (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, max 10MB)
   - **Clinic name**: Your clinic or organization name
   - **Doctor name**: Your professional name
   - **WhatsApp number**: Contact number for communication
   - **Location**: Using Google Maps integration
   - **Bio/Description**: Personal or professional description
   - **Tags**: Add custom tags for categorization
4. Click "Save Changes" for each section

#### 4.2.4 Profile Features

**Bio Section:**
- Add or edit your personal/professional bio
- Supports multi-line text
- Visible to all users viewing your profile

**Tags Section:**
- Add custom tags to categorize your profile
- Tags are saved automatically
- Useful for filtering and searching

**WhatsApp Number:**
- Update your contact number
- Used for order notifications and communication
- Format: +92-XXX-XXXXXXX

**Certificates Section:**
- View all tier achievement certificates
- Certificates are automatically generated when you reach new tiers
- Download certificates as PDF files
- Shows all previous tier certificates, not just current tier

**Research Papers Section:**
- View all your published research papers
- Download your own papers (even if pending approval)
- See views, upvotes, and ratings for each paper

**Badges Section:**
- View achievement badges assigned by admins
- Badges include: Achievement, Milestone, Special, Custom types
- Each badge shows name, description, icon, and earned date

**Rank Progress Section:**
- Live data showing your current tier progress
- Displays:
  - Current tier name
  - Progress percentage to next tier
  - Points to next rank
  - Current score
  - Next rank score
- Updates automatically based on your sales and achievements

#### 4.2.5 Adding Profile Photo

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click on the profile photo area
3. Select an image from your device
4. Crop/resize if needed
5. Click "Save"
6. Your photo will be uploaded and displayed

**Photo Requirements:**
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Maximum size: 10MB (for profile photos)
- Recommended: Square images (1:1 ratio)

**Advertisement Media Requirements:**
- Videos: Maximum 50MB, any length (auto-rotates every 5 seconds)
- Images: Maximum 2MB (JPG, PNG)
- Animations: Maximum 5MB (GIF)

### 4.9 Doctor Appointment System 🆕

The appointment system allows regular users (patients) to connect with doctors for medical consultations.

#### 4.9.1 For Regular Users (Patients)

**Finding and Booking Doctors:**

1. Navigate to **"Set Appointment with Doctors"** from the main menu
2. Browse available doctors:
   - View doctor profiles and specializations
   - See **online status indicators**:
     - 🟢 **Online** - Doctor is currently active
     - 🟡 **Away** - Doctor is temporarily unavailable
     - ⚫ **Offline** - Doctor is not connected
3. Click **"Set Appointment"** on a doctor's card
4. Your request will be sent to the doctor

**Tracking Appointment Status:**

1. Navigate to **"Appointments Status"** (visible to doctors/admins only)
2. Or check the **🔔 Bell icon** for notifications
3. Appointment statuses:
   - **⏳ Waiting** - Request sent, awaiting doctor's response
   - **✅ Confirmed** - Doctor has accepted your request

**When Your Appointment is Accepted:**

- You'll receive a **bell notification**
- You'll receive an **email notification**
- The notification will include doctor's contact information:
  - 📧 Email address
  - 📱 WhatsApp number
  - 🏥 Clinic name
  - 📍 Clinic address

#### 4.9.2 For Doctors

**Receiving Appointment Requests:**

1. When a patient requests an appointment, you'll see:
   - A **🔔 Bell notification** with unread count
   - An **email notification**
2. Navigate to **"Appointments Status"** in the main menu
3. View all pending requests with patient information

**Managing Requests:**

1. Go to **"Appointments Status"**
2. Review pending requests (marked as "New Request")
3. Click **"Accept"** to approve a request
4. Upon acceptance:
   - Patient receives your contact information
   - Patient gets email with your details
   - Status changes to "✅ Confirmed"

**Online Status:**

Your online status is automatically updated based on your activity:
- **🟢 Online** - When you're actively using the platform
- **🟡 Away** - When you've been inactive for a short period
- **⚫ Offline** - When you're not connected

You can also set your availability status:
- **Available** - Open to receiving requests
- **Busy** - Currently occupied
- **Do Not Disturb** - Not accepting new requests

#### 4.9.3 Privacy & Security

- **Private Conversations**: Appointments are only visible to:
  - The specific doctor who received the request
  - The patient who sent the request
  - Platform administrators (for oversight)
- **No Other Doctors**: Other doctors cannot see your appointment requests
- **Contact Info Protection**: Doctor's contact info is only shared after approval

### 4.10 Location Services 🆕

#### 4.10.1 How Location Works

**Privacy-First Approach:**
- We **do NOT** access your browser's geolocation

**What Location is Used For:**
- Finding nearby doctors in your area
- Delivery routing for product orders
- Showing relevant doctors based on proximity

#### 4.10.2 Managing Your Location

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update your location using the map interface
4. Your location is stored securely and never shared without consent

## 6. Support & Help

### 6.1 Frequently Asked Questions

#### 6.1.1 General Questions

**Q: Do I need a Signup ID to register?**
A: Only Doctors need a Signup ID. Regular Users and Employees can register without one.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Doctor approvals are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Regular Users and Employees are auto-approved.

**Q: Can I change my user type after registration?**
A: No, user type cannot be changed after registration. Contact admin if you need to change.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

#### 6.1.2 Order Questions

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Cash on Delivery and PayFast Online Payment are supported.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Delivery time depends on your location and product availability. Contact admin for specific timelines.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Contact admin to cancel orders. Cancellation policies apply.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Go to "My Orders" to view order status and delivery updates.

#### 6.1.3 Research Questions

**Q: Who can upload research papers?**
A: Only Doctors can upload research papers.

**Q: How long does research approval take?**
A: Research papers are typically reviewed within 2-3 business days.

**Q: Can I edit my research paper after submission?**
A: Contact admin to request edits to approved papers.

**Q: What format should research papers be in?**
A: Papers can be written in Markdown format. PDF uploads are also supported.

#### 6.1.4 Tier Questions

**Q: How do I advance to the next tier?**
A: Tiers are based on sales performance. Make orders to increase your tier progress.

**Q: What are the tier benefits?**
A: Benefits include discounts, badges, priority support, and marketing opportunities. See Tier System section for details.

**Q: Can I see my tier progress?**
A: Yes, view your tier progress on your dashboard and profile page.

#### 6.1.5 Profile Questions

**Q: How do I add a profile photo?**
A: Go to your Profile page, click on the photo area, and upload an image.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact admin to change your email address.

**Q: How do I update my location?**
A: Go to Profile → Edit Profile → Update location using Google Maps.
